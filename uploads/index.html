Frequently asked

Questions you probably have.

DoorDash and Uber Eats are built for "I want food in 30 minutes" — they prioritize chains and restaurants. preppa is built for "I want a week of meals from a cook I trust" — we prioritize independent home cooks and weekly subscriptions. Different problem, different product.
preppa is live and adding cooks in new neighborhoods every week. Open the app and set your location to see the Preppas already serving your area. If it's quiet near you for now, leave your email and we'll let you know as more cooks come online — and prioritize neighborhoods with the most interest.
You pay your cook directly for the meals they make. Weekly subscription prices range from about $60 to $200/week depending on the cook and the box size. preppa takes a small fee (10%) from the cook on each order — there's no extra service fee on your end.
Anytime, no questions asked. You can also skip any week, pause your subscription, or switch to a different cook whenever you want.
Every cook on preppa goes through ID verification, food-safety review, and a kitchen inspection before they can take orders. Cooks who offer in-home service (a Preppa visits your kitchen) get additional background checks and insurance verification.
We're onboarding new Preppas in every city we serve. Founding Preppas in a new neighborhood get 0% commission for 60 days, featured placement, and direct support from our founder. Apply at the Preppa section above.
